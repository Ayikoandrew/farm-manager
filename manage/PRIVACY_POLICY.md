# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-01

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected within the Farm Management Platform.

The Platform is built with a commitment to transparency, responsible data use, and user control.

---

## 2. Data Controller

The Platform operator acts as the Data Controller and determines how and why personal and farm-related data is processed, in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (where applicable) and the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019.

---

## 3. Lawful Basis for Data Processing

User data is processed based on:

- **Consent**, provided when users register and use the Platform
- **Legitimate interest**, to operate, maintain, improve, and research the Platform

Data is processed only for purposes directly related to livestock management, system improvement, and applied research.

---

## 4. Data We Collect

The Platform may collect and store:

- Livestock and farm records entered by users
- Operational data required to deliver Platform functionality
- Technical data necessary for system reliability, backup, and synchronization

The Platform does not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information.

---

## 5. How Data Is Used

Data collected through the Platform is used to:

- Provide livestock record management, summaries, and insights
- Perform automatic cloud backup and synchronization
- Improve system reliability, usability, and performance
- Support applied research and train machine learning models aimed at improving livestock management tools

Where data is used for research or machine learning purposes, the focus is on aggregated patterns and system-level behavior rather than individual users or farms.

The Platform does not sell data, share it for advertising purposes, or trade user information.

---

## 6. Research Participation

Users may be given the option to participate in research activities aimed at improving the Platform.

Participation in research does not affect access to core features of the Platform. Where applicable, users may opt out of research participation without losing functionality.

---

## 7. Cloud Sync and Backups

The Platform automatically performs cloud synchronization and backups in the background to prevent data loss and ensure continuity across devices.

Stored data is used strictly for recovery, synchronization, system improvement, and research purposes.

Users may export their data at any time.

---

## 8. Data Ownership

All data entered into the Platform remains the property of the user. The Platform does not claim ownership over livestock, farm, or operational data.

---

## 9. Data Sharing

User data is not shared with third parties, except where required by law.

If data handling practices change in the future, users will be informed.

---

## 10. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored data. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Users are responsible for keeping their access credentials confidential.

---

## 11. User Rights

Users have the right to:

- Access their stored data
- Export their data
- Stop using the Platform at any time

---

## 12. Research and Publications

Data may be used to support academic, technical, or applied research related to livestock management, data systems, and machine learning.

Any publications or presentations based on Platform data will use aggregated information and will not identify individual users or farms.

---

## 13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the Platform evolves. Significant changes will be communicated clearly.
